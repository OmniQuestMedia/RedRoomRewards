# Security Audit and No Backdoor Policy

We are committed to security and transparency in all development practices. This document outlines our policies to ensure a secure software environment.

## Security Audit Process

- **Regular Code Reviews**: Perform scheduled reviews across all modules.
- **Third-party Audits**: Engage external security experts to verify integrity.
- **Penetration Testing**: Conduct tests to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities.

## No Backdoor Policy

- We enforce a zero-tolerance policy for intentional backdoors in code.
- Any unauthorized access mechanisms discovered will prompt immediate remediation and investigation.
- Developers sign agreements to ensure compliance with this policy.

## Transparency Measures

- **Open Reporting**: Encourage public and internal reporting of potential vulnerabilities.
- **Changelog Publication**: Maintain a public changelog for all security-related changes.

Our team believes in fostering trust and building secure software for all stakeholders.
